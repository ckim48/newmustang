    About us

    Hello! We are Mustang Makeover. We hope to make students more aware of over-consumption and waste. Join us in our fight for the future!

    • Looking for a way to help the ASIJ community and commit acts of service? This is where Mustang Makeover comes in. Submit a before and after picture of an area you picked up trash from or cleaned up. Post it on our website to earn a point!
    • Looking for somewhere to donate your old items? We have a solution: Mustang Memories. Submit photos of your old items to our Instagram page for a donation! Find out more about the details on @mustangmakeovers on Instagram.

    Objective

    As students, we have noticed the trash that gets left around campus. Our objective is to clean up the school for a more eco-friendly environment while bringing the students aware of the amount of waste they use.

    Our other objective revolves around the overconsumption at ASIJ. In an age with rapid trends, students buy a lot that they stop using after a couple of months. By establishing a place for students to post items open to other students, we hope to promote a sustainable reusing system.
